Westwind Capital is a European Private Equity Real Estate Investment Management Platform

Westwind Capital’s current investment focus includes Life Sciences & Innovation real estate and the Living sectors, which comprise Purpose Built Student Accommodation, Build-to-Rent, Co-Living and Micro-Living.

We work with a select group of first-class operating partners and seek to provide flexible capital to invest in programmatic long-term relationships and joint ventures in our target sectors.

Sustainability is at the core of how Westwind Capital’s investment ​and asset management process evaluates transactions and developments, and we believe embedding ESG into our business plans from the outset is fundamental to driving long term value for our partners and investors.
