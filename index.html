Westwind Capital is a private equity real estate investment management platform with a pan-European strategy.

Westwind Capital has strong expertise and track-record of investing in specialist alternative real estate sectors across Europe and will typically invest through joint-ventures with local developers and operators. Westwind can also invest throughout different parts of the capital structure.

Sustainability is at the core of how Westwind Capital’s investment ​and asset management process evaluates transactions and developments and we believe embedding ESG into our business plans is fundamental to driving long term value for our partners and investors.

Currently we are investing in Life Science & Innovation real estate and the Living sectors of Purpose Built Student Accommodation, Built-to-Rent and Co-Living and Microliving.
